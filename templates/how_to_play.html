How to Play WordMaster

Master the game in minutes with this complete guide.

🎯 The Goal

Guess the secret word in 6 tries or fewer. Each guess must be a valid English word of the correct length.

🎨 Tile Colors Explained

C
Blue — The letter is in the correct position.
R
Yellow/Amber — The letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
X
Gray — The letter is not in the word at all.

📋 Step-by-Step

  1. Click or type letters to form your first guess.
  2. Press Enter to submit your guess.
  3. Watch the tiles flip to reveal color feedback.
  4. Use the color clues to refine your next guess.
  5. Keep guessing until you find the word or run out of tries!

💡 Pro Tips

  • Start with a word that covers common letters: try CRANE, STARE, or AUDIO.
  • Your second guess should target letters you haven't tried yet.
  • Pay attention to yellow tiles — the letter is there, just in a different spot.
  • Common word endings: -ING, -TION, -ED, -ER.
  • Common word starters: S-, ST-, TR-, CH-.
