The Basics

WordMaster is a word-guessing game. Your goal is to find the hidden word in 6 tries or fewer.

  • Each guess must be a real English word of the correct length.
  • After each guess, colored tiles reveal how close you are.
  • Use the clues from each guess to narrow down the answer.
  • The game is won when all tiles turn blue — correct letters, correct positions.
What the Colors Mean
C R A N E

🟦 Blue — Correct
The letter is in the word and in the right position.

S T A R E

🟨 Yellow — Wrong position
The letter is in the word but not in that spot. Move it!

A U D I O

⬜ Gray — Not in word
The letter does not appear in the word at all. Eliminate it.

Winning Strategy Guide
🚀 Best Opening Words

Cover the most common letters (E, A, R, I, O, T, N, S) with your first guess:

CRANE STARE AUDIO RAISE AROSE
🔍 Common Patterns

English words follow predictable structures:

  • Endings: -ING, -TION, -ED, -ER, -LY
  • Starters: S-, ST-, TR-, CH-, SH-, PL-
  • Common pairs: TH, SH, CH, OU, EA, AI
🧠 Think Systematically
  • After guess 1, use guess 2 to test new letters.
  • Yellow letters must move — never reuse the same position.
  • Once you have 3+ blue tiles, try filling the gaps logically.
⚠️ Common Mistakes
  • Don't reuse letters already confirmed as gray.
  • Don't ignore yellow tiles — they are vital clues.
  • Avoid guessing obscure words when common ones fit better.
