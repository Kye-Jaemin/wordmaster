How to Play WordMaster

WordMaster is a free, browser-based word guessing game inspired by classic word puzzles. Your goal is simple: guess the hidden 5-letter word within 6 attempts. After each guess, the tiles change color to tell you how close you are.

  • A green tile means the letter is correct and in the right position.
  • A yellow tile means the letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
  • A grey tile means the letter is not in the word at all.

Every guess must be a real, valid English word. Use the on-screen keyboard or your physical keyboard to type your guesses. You can use the Hint button if you are truly stuck — it reveals one letter to get you back on track.

The best strategy is to start with a word that covers common letters. Words like CRANE, AUDIO, or STARE give you the widest coverage of frequently used letters in the English language. From there, eliminate possibilities with each guess and zero in on the answer.

All WordMaster Game Modes

WordMaster goes far beyond a single daily puzzle. Whether you want a quick warm-up, an educational challenge, or unlimited practice, there is a mode designed for you.

📅 Daily Challenge

One new word every day, shared by all players worldwide. Compare your solve with friends and track your winning streak over time. The daily word resets at midnight.

♾️ Unlimited Mode

No limits — play as many rounds as you like with a fresh random word each time. Perfect for practice sessions, language learners building fluency, or anyone who finishes the daily puzzle and wants more.

😊 Easy Mode (4 Letters)

A gentler challenge with 4-letter words. Great for younger players, beginners, or anyone who wants to build confidence before tackling the standard 5-letter game.

🔥 Hard Mode (6 Letters)

Step up the difficulty with 6-letter words. Longer words mean exponentially more possible combinations — designed for experienced players who want a serious mental workout.

📰 News Word Challenge

Read today's BBC World News headlines, then guess the 5-letter word hidden within them. Real news, real vocabulary — you learn words in their actual context, which dramatically improves retention.

🐾 Category Modes

Focused vocabulary practice with themed word sets — Animals, Food, and more. Category modes are ideal for students, ESL learners, and anyone preparing for specific vocabulary exams.

Why Play Word Games Every Day?

Daily word puzzles are one of the most effective ways to keep your brain sharp. Research in cognitive science shows that word games activate multiple brain systems simultaneously — pattern recognition, working memory, executive function, and lexical retrieval. Playing regularly strengthens the neural pathways that support language and reasoning.

Unlike passive vocabulary study (reading a word list), guessing games force active retrieval — you have to produce an answer, not just recognize one. Active retrieval creates far stronger memory traces and leads to genuine, lasting vocabulary growth. This is why WordMaster is used by SAT and IELTS test-takers alongside recreational players.

WordMaster is completely free to use, requires no account, and works on any device. Open it in your browser, play your game, and come back tomorrow. No installation, no subscription, no catches.
