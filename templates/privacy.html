Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

WordMaster does not require account registration. We do not collect personal identifying information such as your name, email, or address.

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized usage data (pages visited, session duration, device type) to help improve the game. This data does not identify you personally.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your game statistics (games played, win streak) locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser and is not sent to our servers.

Third-party advertising (Google AdSense) may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

3. Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show personalized ads based on your browsing history. WordMaster does not control which ads are displayed.

4. Third-Party Links

Our blog and pages may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: privacy@wordmaster-game.com
