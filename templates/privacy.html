Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

This policy describes how WordMaster ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use wordmaster.store.

TL;DR — We don't sell your data. We don't require an account. Game stats live only on your device. Ads are served by Google AdSense. Analytics are anonymous.
1. Information We Collect
1a. Information you provide

WordMaster does not require you to create an account. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or postal address through normal gameplay.

If you choose to contact us via email or the contact form, we will receive your name and email address solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add these to any mailing list.

1b. Automatically collected information

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized usage data, including pages visited, session duration, device type, and general geographic region (country/city level). This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. IP addresses are anonymized by Google.

2. Cookies & Local Storage
2a. Local Storage (first-party)

We use your browser's localStorage to store your game statistics (games played, win streak, guess distribution) entirely on your own device. This data is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time via your browser settings.

2b. Third-party cookies

Google AdSense and Google Analytics may place cookies on your browser to serve relevant advertisements and to measure site traffic. These cookies are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out via:

  • Google Ad Settings — opt out of personalised ads
  • Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
3. Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show personalised ads based on your browsing history across websites. WordMaster earns a small advertising fee to keep the site free — we do not control which specific ads are displayed.

To learn more, visit Google's How AdSense Works page.

4. Third-Party Services
Service Purpose Data shared
Google AdSense Advertising Cookie / interest data (by Google)
Google Analytics 4 Anonymous traffic analysis Anonymised IP, device, behavior
Free Dictionary API In-game word definitions & hints The word you are playing (no personal data)
Bootstrap CDN / jsDelivr UI styling & scripts Your IP (standard HTTP request)
5. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers in connection with gameplay. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default. Any email you send us is kept only as long as needed to resolve your query.

Your game stats stored in localStorage remain on your device until you clear your browser data.

6. Children's Privacy

WordMaster is a general-audience word game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently received information from a child, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights (EEA / UK / California)

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Because we hold minimal personal data, most requests can be resolved by clearing your browser's localStorage. For any other data (e.g. email correspondence), contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

California residents: WordMaster does not sell personal information within the meaning of the CCPA.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of WordMaster after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact
