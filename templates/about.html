About WordMaster

The free, browser-based word guessing game — no login, no app, just words.

What is WordMaster?

WordMaster is a free, browser-based word guessing game. No account, no download, no subscription required — just pure word-puzzle fun accessible from any device.

Guess the hidden word in 6 tries. After each guess, the colored tiles reveal how close you are. Sharpen your vocabulary and challenge your friends!

Game Modes

  • Daily Daily Challenge — One new word every day. Compare your score with others!
  • ∞ Unlimited Mode — No limit. Play as many rounds as you want.
  • Easy Easy (4 letters) — Perfect for beginners or younger players.
  • Hard Hard (6 letters) — For word masters seeking an extra challenge.
  • 🐾🍕 Category Modes — Animals, food, and more themed word sets.
