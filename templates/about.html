About WordMaster

The free, browser-based word guessing game.

What is WordMaster?

WordMaster is a free, browser-based word guessing game. No account, no download, no subscription required — just pure word-puzzle fun accessible from any device.

Guess the hidden word in 6 tries. After each guess, the colored tiles will reveal how close you are. Sharpen your vocabulary and challenge your friends!

Game Modes

  • Daily Challenge — One new word every day. Compare your score with others!
  • Unlimited Mode — No limit. Play as many rounds as you want.
  • Easy (4 letters) — Perfect for beginners or younger players.
  • Hard (6 letters) — For word masters seeking an extra challenge.
  • Category Modes — Animals, food, and more themed word sets.

Legal Note

WordMaster is an independent project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the New York Times or the original Wordle game. The word-guessing game format is a classic puzzle type with no single owner.
